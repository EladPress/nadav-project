Lemon pie recipe

Ingredients for almond dough

  • 200g flour
  • 175g Grounded Almonds
  • 174g sugar powder
  • 175g Coconut oil
  • 1 egg

    1. Add all the ingredients to the mixer and mix until
      becomes a soft, sand-like dough that retains its shape
      when pressed into shape (if this does not happen, you can add more coconut oil)

    2. The dough is soft and cannot be rolled, so press it evenly in a tart pan

    3. Bake at 180 degrees Celsius for 15 minutes

    Ingredients for the lemon filling

    • 4 egg
    • 1 cup sugar
    • 0.5 cup lemon juice
    • 1 tabespoon lemon zest
    • 0.25 all-purpose flour
    1. Mix all the ingredients together in a bowl
    2. When the dough is ready, gently pour the filling and cook for another 15 minutes
    3. After 15 minutes, check with a knife if the filling is ready
      (prick with a knife when it is clean, it is ready)
    4. You can eat it hot,
      it is recommended to wait 30 minutes
    5. enjoy!!
